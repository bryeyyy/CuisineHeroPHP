What is CuisineHero?

CuisineHero aims to connect Flipinos through our love of food and cooking, in a smart and intuitive manner. The objective of CuisineHero is to provide a platform for Filipinos to find, share and interact with other users through cooking and sharing our modifications to the recipes of our favorite filipino dishes. A Smart Filipino cookbook, fusing online cookbooks that we know and love with the ability for the users to share their own recipes for others to view and try. CuisineHero contains recipes of our favorite filipino dishes from the different regions of the Philippines, and these are meant to be expanded overtime through users‚Äô own recipes with the built-in social feed.

Who we are:

Team Hello World;

Hello World is a group of visionary entrepreneurs willing to revolutionize our everyday gastronomic activities through CuisineHero. We know how important tradition is when it comes to food preparation as we are also once spoiled with delicious local delicacies and local variations of famous world dishes by our loved ones.

CuisineHero is an idea that arose from the struggles of our youth in replicating the genius and ingenious methods of our exemplars while we are subjected to low socioeconomic conditions and maximum time constraints.

We at Hello World offers CuisineHero to anyone wishing to enjoy food preparation without having to struggle much in the kitchen. Ideas can be shared, ideas can be discovered, and every user will surely enjoy without having to worry much about the methods.

By your participation and invitations, CuisineHero will be able to expand its user-based library of recipes and methods. We will surely never run out of ways to cook!
