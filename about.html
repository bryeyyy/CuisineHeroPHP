What is CuisineHero?

CuisineHero aims to connect Flipinos through our love of food and cooking, in a smart and intuitive manner. The objective of CuisineHero is to provide a platform for Filipinos to find, share and interact with other users through cooking and sharing our modifications to the recipes of our favorite filipino dishes. A Smart Filipino cookbook, fusing online cookbooks that we know and love with the ability for the users to share their own recipes for others to view and try. CuisineHero contains recipes of our favorite filipino dishes from the different regions of the Philippines, and these are meant to be expanded overtime through users‚Äô own recipes with the built-in social feed.
